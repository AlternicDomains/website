Domain Name Broker

We acquire, negotiate, and sell premium domain names but also social media handles, blockchain names (ENS, SOL), and NFT-based assets.

Why Use a Domain Broker?

High-value naming assets require discretion, market intelligence, and structured execution. Alternic acts as a neutral, trusted intermediary—protecting anonymity, validating ownership, and structuring secure transfers.

Pricing & Engagement

We offer success-based brokerage, retainer + performance hybrid, or exclusive mandate structures depending on asset class, value tier, and complexity.

Standard success fee: 1% of the final deal value (minimum €50 / $50). Higher-complexity, portfolio, or stealth retained mandates can be quoted on a custom structure (e.g. reduced % + retainer).

Payment methods: We can settle brokerage fees and transaction facilitation costs via traditional bank transfer (SWIFT/SEPA) or in approved digital assets, including major stablecoins (e.g. USDC, USDT, EURC) and leading cryptocurrencies (e.g. Bitcoin BTC, Ethereum ETH, Tron, Solana, Ton...). On‑chain settlement is coordinated through reputable escrow or custody workflows. (Compliance note: where required, KYC/AML checks may apply prior to accepting crypto-based settlement.)
