Domain Name Custody & Trustee Service

Custody domain name, custodian domain name, trustee domain name — Secure, anonymous, and expert management for your valuable digital assets.

If you as an individual, company, or institution don't want to own a domain directly, Alternic can own the domain name on your behalf as a trusted custodian or trustee.

Use Cases:

  • Remain anonymous for operational security
  • Share ownership between two or more parties
  • Delegate negotiations for domain sales (we handle all inquiries and negotiations)
  • Delegate management: high security, renewals, privacy (no public WHOIS info)

It's better to trust an expert to manage your domain. Individuals or companies not specialized in domain management may miss critical security steps or forget renewals, risking loss of the domain.

Alternic (Account SAS) is a French registered company. We can custody domain names, as well as Web3 names like ENS (.eth).
